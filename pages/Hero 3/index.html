Nevermore

The Shadow Fiend

Necromastery

Nevermore gains souls from enemy hero kills and creep kills, increasing his damage and attack speed.

Shadowraze

Nevermore sweeps an area in front of him with a powerful shadowraze, dealing damage to all enemies caught in the area.

Presence of the Dark Lord

Nevermore's presence causes nearby enemy units to have reduced movement speed and attack speed.

Requiem of Souls

Nevermore unleashes a powerful requiem, dealing damage to all enemies based on the number of souls he has collected.
