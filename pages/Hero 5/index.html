Drought

Groudon's Drought ability intensifies sunlight, weakening Water-type moves and strengthening Fire-type moves.

Groudon slams the ground, causing massive spikes to erupt from the earth, damaging all surrounding foes.

Earthquake

Groudon creates a powerful earthquake, dealing massive damage to all grounded foes.
