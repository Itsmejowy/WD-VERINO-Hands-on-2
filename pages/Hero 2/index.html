Kagura - The Onmyoji Master

KAGURA
The Onmyoji Master

"I am the light that protects the peace of the world."

Abilities:

  • Seimei Umbrella: Unique ability to control her umbrella.
  • Seimei's Mark: Leaves a mark on the enemy that explodes.
  • Yin Yang Overturn: Summons lightning.

Lore:

Kagura is a mystical mage from the East, known as the Onmyouji Master. She wields the power of the Yin and Yang, using her enchanted umbrella to cast powerful spells and protect the world from darkness.
