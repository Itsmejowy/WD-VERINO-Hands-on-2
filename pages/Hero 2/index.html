Kagura - The Onmyoji Master

KAGURA
The Onmyoji Master

"I am the light that protects the peace of the world."

About Kagura

Kagura is a powerful mage from the Land of the Rising Sun. She wields the Seimei Umbrella, which was passed down from her ancestors. With her exceptional skills in onmyodo, she can control the balance between yin and yang, protecting her allies and defeating her enemies.

Skills

  • Seimei Umbrella Open: Kagura throws her Seimei Umbrella to a target location, dealing magic damage to enemies hit.
  • Rasho Umbrella Flee: Kagura leaves her umbrella behind and gains a shield, increasing her movement speed.
  • Yin Yang Overturn: Kagura deals massive magic damage to enemies around her umbrella, pulling them towards it and stunning them.
