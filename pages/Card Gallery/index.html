Yasuo - The Unforgiven

Yasuo is a highly mobile melee carry champion in League Of Legends, known for his ability to outplay opponents with his swift movements and deadly sword strikes.

Wielding the power of the wind, Yasuo can dash through enemies, create a whirlwind to knock them up, and even unleash a powerful ultimate ability that slices through multiple foes.

His playstyle revolves around carefully managing his dashes and windwall to create openings for devastating attacks. Mastering Yasuo requires a combination of mechanical skill, map awareness, and strategic decision-making.

Viper

Valorant's Toxic Agent

About Viper

Viper is a Controller agent in Valorant, known for her ability to control and manipulate toxic gas. She can create a dense smoke screen, launch a poisonous cloud, and even deploy a deadly viper's pit that slows and damages enemies.

Viper's abilities make her a formidable agent, especially in close-quarters combat and on maps with tight corridors. Her versatility allows her to support her team in various ways, from denying vision to setting up aggressive plays.

Toxic Screen

Viper can create a dense smoke screen to deny vision and control areas of the map.

Poison Cloud

Viper can launch a poisonous cloud that slows and damages enemies caught within it.

Viper's Pit

Viper can deploy a deadly viper's pit that slows and damages enemies trapped inside.

Nevermore's presence causes nearby enemy units to have reduced movement speed and attack speed.

Requiem of Souls

Nevermore unleashes a powerful requiem, dealing damage to all enemies based on the number of souls he has collected.
